Browsing / Searching for a Module

Click Module Signup on the left hand side navigation menu or at the top of the page.The welcome page will open with a Navigation Tree of the Divisions and other training providers in the University. To browse the modules:

  1. Click on the Division of interest to expand the list of Departments within the Division.
  2. Click on the Department in order to expand the list of Modules being offered by that Department.
  3. Modules in green are available for SignUp, Modules in black are not available to Signup and Modules in red are full.
  4. Modules in green also contain the number of days left before the course is closed for signup.
  5. Click on an individual Module in the navigation tree to display the corresponding Module information on the right.
  6. Click the signup button if you wish to be enrolled for the course.

Alternatively you may wish to search for a module:

  1. Above the navigation tree in module signup there are links relating to sections within the Student Enrolment System, including Module Signup, Module Search, and My Modules. Click on Module Search.
  2. A list of courses will appear in the main body of the page. These courses are currently open for booking and can be browsed as one long list by scrolling to the bottom of the page and clicking the link 'show all results'. To find out more about a course, click the button 'More details' under each result.
  3. There are a number of options to search for a module on the right hand side of the page. To search by a specific word or term, use the search box provided at the top. Results of this search will be listed by relevance on the main body of the page.
  4. To search by department, click on the department name under the main search box. A tick will appear by the department's name and a list of courses will display in the main body of the page. Clicking on more than one department will add results relevant to that department.
  5. Courses can also be searched by their 'bookable' status. Underneath the departmental filters are three links - Bookable 'Later', 'Yes', and 'No'. By clicking later, a list appears in the main body of the page for those courses on the system that are not yet ready to sign-up.By clicking yes, a list of currently open courses appears. By clicking no, a list of courses which are now closed will appear.
