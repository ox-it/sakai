Withdrawing from a Course

This only applies to courses that can be booked in this tool - this means courses offered by Social Sciences and MPLS.

Check the status of your application by clicking on My Courses and following the corresponding instructions below.

If your status on the Course is PENDING

  1. Click on My Courses at the top of the page.
  2. In the status column next to the Course you wish to withdraw from will be link labelled 'Withdraw'. By selecting this you will be WITHDRAWN.
  3. An email will be sent to both the Course Administrator and your Supervisor to notify them of your withdrawal.

If your status on the Course is APPROVED

  1. If you have been APPROVED on a Course you will need to email the Course Administrator in order to withdraw. The details of the Course Administrator are found on the acceptance email you received.
