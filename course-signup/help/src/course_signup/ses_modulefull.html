What to do if a Course is Full

This only applies to courses that can be booked in this tool - this means courses offered by Social Sciences and MPLS.

If you find a course you would like to enrol for but it is full you have the option of being added to a waiting list. In the course you are trying to enrol for, click on the Join Waiting List button which will prompt you to add your supervisor's email and reasons for attending the course. If a place becomes available on the course you will receive an email confirmation offering you a place from the Course Administrator.
