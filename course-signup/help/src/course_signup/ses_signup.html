Signing Up for a Course

Signing Up for a Social Sciences or MPLS Course

This is only relevant for courses run by the Social Sciences or MPLS divisions.

Once you have found a course that you would like to attend, please carefully check that you fulfil the eligibility criteria to make sure the course is suitable for you. Please also ensure that you are able to attend the course on the date and times specified.

Note: If the course is marked Full then read What to do if a Course is Full.

  1. Select the course parts you wish attend by clicking the radio button next to the option which suits you - this will be made clear in the description (for example, you may be required to sign up for both a Laboratory and Lecture series). In many cases there will be only one course part to select. Click the grey Signup button at the bottom of the course description.

  2. A pop up window will ask for your supervisor's email address and your reasons for wanting to attend the Course. This is needed because your supervisor's permission is required for you to attend any training Courses. To continue, click 'Confirm Signup' or 'Cancel' if you no longer wish to enrol for the course.

    Note: You will need to enter an email address recognised by the University.

  3. Following submission an email will be sent to your Supervisor and the Course Administrator for approval.

  4. When approval has been granted you will be notified via email that you have a place on the Course. You will also be notified if approval is not granted.

Signing Up for a Course Offered by the Other Training Providers

Once you have found a course that you would like to attend, please carefully check that you fulfil the eligibility criteria to make sure the course is suitable for you. Please also ensure that you are able to attend the course on the date and times specified.

Click on the hyperlink given at the bottom of the page, this will take you to the training provider's website. Depending on the course that was selected, you may need to supply a username and password.
