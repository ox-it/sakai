MPLS Course Welcome and Sign Up Instructions

Welcome to the MPLS Division’s course sign up page. These courses are open to all students in the Division who meet each individual course’s eligibility criteria. These criteria are set out in the course descriptions and you should not try to register for any course for which you are not eligible.

To register for a Divisional course:

  1. Select the course you wish to attend from the list provided under ‘MPLS Division’ on the left hand side of the screen.
  2. Carefully check the course description and eligibility criteria to make sure the course is suitable for you.
  3. Click the ‘Signup’ button at the bottom of the course description.
  4. A pop up window will ask for your supervisor’s email address and ask for your reasons for wanting to attend the course. This is needed because your supervisor’s permission is required for you to attend any training courses.
  5. Following submission an email will be sent to your supervisor and the course administrator for approval.
  6. When approval has been granted you will be notified via email that you have a place on the course.
  7. You will also be notified if approval is not granted.

If you have queries about the Divisional Transferable Skills courses, please contact skillscourses@mpls.ox.ac.uk.

To browse and register for courses in other departments, click on that department in the list to the left of the screen.

Searching for a course will display results from the currently highlighted Division only.

If you have questions about the Graduate Academic Programme, please contact gap@mpls.ox.ac.uk.
